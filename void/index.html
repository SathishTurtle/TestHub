A lot has changed over the last 50 years, but the legacy of PUMA Suede has remained constant. The Suede hit streets, courts and curbs in 1968 and has been changing the game ever since. Donned by legends across decades, the Suede is of iconic silhouette, classic design and bold attitude.

In a creative endeavour that captures the grit of Indian streets, Suede is the coming together of a collective of artists who have found inspiration from the streets they come; whose art is authentic, unadulterated and rich with soul. Like all things Indian, this collaboration is as diverse as can be, featuring 4 languages, 8 rappers, 36 dancers, 7 street artists and shot across 4 corners of the country.

Set to be India’s largest street creative collaboration, Suede Gully pays homage to the cultural relevance of Suede, a sneaker that represents the spirit of every generation
